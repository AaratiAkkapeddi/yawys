Does Your Browser Know Who You Are?

In 2011, the Google Data Liberation Front released Google Takeout, a service that allows users to download some of their Google app data like browser history, emails, or search history. This data once downloaded, comes in the form of a .json file which is difficult for the average user to parse through. The goal of this google search visualizer is to provide you with an easy to use tool to play through your search history chronologically and to be able to pause and reflect on why you were searching what you did at that time.
